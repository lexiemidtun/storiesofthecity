Metamorphoses (43 B.C.E.- 17 C.E.)

Ovid

Metamorphosis is one of my favorite texts I read during HON 171. We read five stories for class: “The Creation” (Book I), “Apollo and Daphne” (Book I), “Jove and Io” (Book II), “Iphis and Isis” (Book IX), and “Pygmalion” (Book X). While I enjoyed all of these stories, I have chosen to discuss the first, second, and fourth below, as I found “The Creation” and “Apollo and Daphne” to resonate with me the most and “Iphis and Isis” to bring forth the most questions.
